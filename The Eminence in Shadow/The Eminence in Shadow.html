The Eminence in Shadow

Season 1

The Eminence in Shadow is a Japanese light novel series written by Daisuke Aizawa and illustrated by Tōzai. It began serialization online in May 2018 on the user-generated novel publishing website Shōsetsuka ni Narō. It was later acquired by Enterbrain, who have published the series since November 2018.

A manga adaptation with art by Anri Sakano has been serialized in Kadokawa Shoten's seinen manga magazine Comp Ace since December 2018. A spin-off manga by Seta U titled Kage no Jitsuryokusha ni Naritakute! Shadow Gaiden has also been serialized via Comp Ace since July 2019 . Both the light novel and main manga have been licensed in North America by Yen Press. An anime television series adaptation produced by Nexus aired from October 2022 to February 2023. A second season is set to premiere in October 2023.

Cid Kagenō biography

Cid Kagenō, formerly Minoru Kagenō, is the titular antihero and main protagonist of The Eminence in Shadow series. He is the leader of the secret organization Shadow Garden under the moniker Shadow, which he founded to live out his chūnibyō fantasies, unaware that his imaginary enemies actually exist.

Description of the animated series

This is a continuation of the story about a young guy named Kai who strives to become the most powerful ninja in his world. Kai had recently become part of the ninja clan, but he had always remained in the shadow of his more talented comrades, who received all the attention and accolades. But Kai does not give up, he continues to train and improve his skills, dreaming of becoming the best ninja. This season, Kai sets off with his clan on a dangerous adventure to stop an evil ninja who is trying to take over the world and destroy everyone who stands in his way.
